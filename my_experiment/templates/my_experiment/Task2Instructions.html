Task 2: Intertemporal choice

For the second task, you will be faced with 5 decisions. For each decision (1 through 5), you are asked to allocate tokens between two payment dates:

  • Earlier (today)
  • Later (in 3 weeks)

You have a budget of 100 tokens in each decision, and you can split them however you like.

Token values
  • Each token allocated to today is worth 1 × token.
    Example: If you put 10 tokens on today, you receive 10 tokens.
  • Each token allocated to 3 weeks from now is worth more than 1 × token.
    Example: If the rate is 1.5 and you put 10 tokens on later, in 3 weeks you get 10 × 1.5 = 15 tokens.

At the end of the experiment, one of the five decisions will be randomly selected for payment. You will receive the token split you made in that decision as your bonus.

Important: You must complete all 5 decisions before you can proceed.

When you are ready to begin, click the arrow at the bottom right of the screen to proceed to Task 2.
